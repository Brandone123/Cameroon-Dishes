About Us

Oslo Diner is a restaurant located in the heart of Dang, Ngaoundéré. We operate a unique system that allows you to book reservations and meals in advance up to as little as one hour before your arrival and be served immediately you arrive.
Our menu covers a vast array of local recipes of our country and is tailored to adapt to the ever dynamic taste of our customers. This means we have a flexible menu to accommodate the different desires of our customers at very affordable prices, because at Oslo Diner, “your taste is our command”.
Our 200 seater restaurant also doubles as an event center and we have the best event management and catering team on ground to make your dream event a reality.
